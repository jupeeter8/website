Happy New year friends. I know I am a little too late to the task, still, i hope you have a great one.
I have been spending much time on my computer and hanging out with friends so I was procrastinating
writing. Also I did not know what to write. I have been doing a lot but none of it feels important.

I have made this a small update note since a friend asked me when I would write again.  
I have returned to Bangalore and I mostly spend my time tweaking my Linux installation. It's something
I had wanted to do for a very long time but it's only since this year that I have found the time to do
it. I used to dread it becuase of the excessive documnets it requires you to go through that in the end
do not work. Since I don't have any _work work_ these days, I can't really come up with a good excuse
to no do it.  
When I get bored I go out and rope my friends into hanging out with me. It's very easy now since I have
come back after a while so everyone says yes, but tbh it's just something I have always done since childhood.
I don't like being home so I keep roping everyone into hanging out with me.

I remember during one of the last few days of my work, I had not gone to work and I was so bored that
I asked AK47 to come to Ikea with me. I am happy when friends are around. 2025 was great in a lot of
ways but I specially like it becuase I was able to catch with almost all of my friends somehow. For me
that's a monumental task. I spent some time in december with _MDVC_ and _D_, always
fun. I also met _Roomate_ (form college) after a year. I stay most upto date with him but we hardly ever
meet. During christmas he visited me at my home and I had great time showing him my terrible
town and discussing wether it is a tier-3 city or not. I chose to come back to banglaore on my bike
so I also had the opportunity to meet _Guchi_ on the way. So 2025 was great becuase I got to meet most
of my friends from college and school and then some that I had never met before. I also realized that
all of us are very different now and no one can do anything about it.

Ride back to Bangalore was fun. I got to put everything I learnt from my previous rides to use so
I hardly had any problems. I rode for much longer and met a guy on the way to Hyderabad
who rode with me till I reached my destination. A much better experience than my last time.

That's pretty much it. There are a lot of things I want to do before the year ends so I'll mostly be
spending my time towards that once I get out of my holiday mode.
